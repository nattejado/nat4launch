Scoring methodology (click to expand)

Each deal is scored 0–10 across five Launch-thesis dimensions. Final score is a weighted average. Heuristics are intentionally transparent — the goal is fast triage of inbound + cold sourcing, not autonomous decisions.

  • Stage fit (25%): Is this a pre-seed/seed where Launch's $25K–$500K check is welcome?
  • Software / SMB lean (20%): Software-first, SMB- or prosumer-facing? Launch dislikes hard biotech / heavy infra.
  • AI-native (20%): Is AI core to the wedge? Agentic > model-thin wrapper.
  • Founder signal (20%): Domain depth, prior exits, network density, builder energy.
  • Inbound vector (15%): Can Launch credibly meet them? Austin proximity, personal warm intro, accelerator pedigree.
